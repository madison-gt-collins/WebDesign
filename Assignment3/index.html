My Policeman

A Novel by Bethan Roberts


The story of a love triangle between two men and a woman, and a forbidden love between the two men in 1957 in Britain
My Policeman

The First Word of the Book

I considered starting with these words: I no longer want to kill you—because I really don’t—but then decided you would think this far too melodramatic.
You’ve always hated melodrama, and I don’t want to upset you now, not in the state you’re in, not at what may be the end of your life.
